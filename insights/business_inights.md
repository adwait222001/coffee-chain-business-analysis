## 2.Financial Insights

*Based on the first query of indepth_exploration we can get ingredient level economics of each ingredient across all stores like its sales,
cost of good sold(cogs),gross margin per ingredient ,expenses and profit.
Based on it we can conclude that Columbian coffee has good amount of sales and has higher margins which may result in highest profitability among all products.

*Based on the second and third queries, we can identify the top and second-top stores based on sales in each state, along with their top-selling products. These insights can be used to develop future business strategies and improve sales performance.
*Based on the fourth and fifth queries, we can observe that the country is divided into four sectors: East, West, Central, and South. The fourth query provides insights into total sales and profit per sector, while the fifth query shows the distribution of expenses such as marketing and other expenses across all sectors. This comparison enables analysis between revenue (sales and profit) and operational costs (expenses) across different regions.

*Based on sixth query identifies the top 3 most operationally profitable stores within each state by ranking stores based on their operating profit. Using this output, we can compare how operational costs (COGS, marketing, and other expenses) impact profitability at the store level across different states, and evaluate which locations are managing costs more efficiently while generating higher operating profits.

*Based on the seventh query, the West market records the highest total COGS, followed by Central, East, and South. This indicates that the West market incurs the largest product-related costs, which may be driven by higher sales volumes, larger inventory requirements, or greater operational scale. By comparing total COGS across markets, we can assess cost distribution patterns and identify regions where procurement, production, or inventory management activities are more intensive, providing insight into the markets that contribute most significantly to overall business costs.
